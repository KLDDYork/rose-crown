Main Menu.

All of our food is cooked from fresh to order using locally sourced meats and vegetables. As a result of this, we require tables with 10 or more people to pre-book and pre-order. For tables of 10+ people who do neither, we cannot guarantee that you will be served as a group, this will depend on how busy the pub is at the time and it is more likely that you will be served as individuals.
