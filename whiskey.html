WHISKEY.

Here at the Rose & Crown we are proud of offering an extensive range of whisky, not just from Scotland but around the world including Taiwan, India, France (beleive it or not) and of course the ever growing & popular Japanese whiskies.

The Spirit of Yorkshire Maturing malt.

Come and try our new addition to the bar, the eagerly awaited first malt to be distilled and released in Yorkshire
(Hunmanby to be precise)

Visit Site.

We currently stock up to 70 single malt whiskies but also a few interesting unusual blends such as the Great King Street blended whisky from the guys at Compass Box.

We stock all the classics such as the easy drinking, mellow Glenmorangie 10 year old & the classic Islay Laphroaig 10 year old which is packed full of peat and smoke, not one for the faint hearted.

We are keen to have something a little off the beaten track too, ones you may not see in many places such as the Hepburns Choice specially selected single cask from the Braeval Distillery.
